My Master of Public Health capstone project focuses on addressing critical public health challenges through community-based research and evidence-based interventions. This project represents the culmination of my graduate studies in public health and demonstrates my commitment to advancing health equity and improving population health outcomes.

<!--more-->

## Project Status

**Current Status:** In Development

**Expected Completion:** May 2026

---

## Project Focus Areas

This capstone project will explore:

### Population Health & Epidemiology
Analyzing health disparities and social determinants of health to identify intervention opportunities within underserved communities.

### Community-Based Interventions
Developing and implementing evidence-based public health programs that address specific community health needs and promote health equity.

### Health Policy & Advocacy
Examining policy frameworks and advocacy strategies that support sustainable public health improvements and systemic change.

### Data-Driven Decision Making
Utilizing epidemiological methods and biostatistics to inform program design, implementation, and evaluation.

---

## Anticipated Deliverables

Upon completion, this project will include:

- **Comprehensive Needs Assessment** - Community health profile, gap analysis, and stakeholder engagement findings

- **Intervention Design & Implementation** - Evidence-based program framework, implementation strategy, and evaluation plan

- **Data Analysis & Findings** - Quantitative and qualitative analysis, key outcomes and impact measures, and policy recommendations

- **Final Report & Presentation** - Executive summary, full research documentation, and community presentation materials

## Methodology

The project will utilize a mixed-methods approach:

### Quantitative Methods
- Secondary data analysis of public health surveillance data
- Statistical analysis of health outcomes
- Program evaluation metrics

### Qualitative Methods
- Community stakeholder interviews
- Focus group discussions
- Case study analysis

### Community-Based Participatory Research
- Collaborative partnership with community organizations
- Community input in research design
- Shared decision-making throughout the project
